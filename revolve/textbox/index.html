You look at the Project Re:VOLVE textbox generator.

There’s no way to download your creation yet, so what I do is hit the print screen key, paste into GIMP, use Rectangle Select Tool to select only the textbox, select Image → Crop to Selection, then Image → Crop to Content, use Select by Color Tool to select one of the borders (in Tool Options, make sure Threshold is 0), then hit the delete key to erase all of them, making the textbox transparent. In GIMP 2.10 and earlier, you may have to export to maintain transparency, as copying it to your clipboard may make it black again. If you plan on uploading to social media, you may want to zoom in first to fight against lossy compression.

The font was redrawn by hand using BitFontMaker 2.
It has the same characters as the original bitmap font, plus caret (^); smart quotes aren’t included.
On mobile, you may have to hold your quote keys until straight quotes appear.
Download: TTF, WOFF2, WOFF
